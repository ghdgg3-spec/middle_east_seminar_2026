Event Overview
This seminar is held during Medical Korea 2026 (2026.3.19 – 3.22) and provides a space for academic exchange and networking among trainees, faculty members, and related organizations.

Academic Exchange

Faculty sharing training guidance experiences and presenting latest clinical trends.

Experience Sharing

Trainees presenting their medical training experiences in Korea.

Networking

Free networking session including a Lucky Draw.

Purpose
Strengthen academic exchange and expand professional networks centered around the Middle East medical training program.
Academic exchange Facilitate academic interaction between training faculty and trainees.
Clinical trends Share latest clinical skills, techniques, and medical trends.
Network activation Enhance networking among related organizations and training stakeholders.
